Quantum Dynamics

Here are some codes I produced. None of them are original. They are attemps to replicate the literature results, and are heavily inspired by my supervisor Prof. Stuart Althorpe.
  • Ring polymer molecular dynamics for Kubo-transformed correlation functions: this aims to replicate the results from I. Craig and D. Manolopoulos (2004)
  • Ring polymer molecular dynamics for Reaction Rates: this aims to replicate the results from I. Craig and D. Manolopoulos (2005)
  • Quantum Instanton: this aims to calculate the quantum scattering rate over a 1D Eckart barrier. References includes Miller et al (2003) and Yamamoto and Miller (2004), but the sampling methods are completely different.
